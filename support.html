ELECTRO MEDICAL DEPARTMENT

Brief History of EM Department

The Electro medical Department of POF Hospital is working under the supervision & guidance of Director Services since 1986 and headed by an Officer of Asst. Manager rank and also providing repair / maintenance services for all types of biomedical equipment installed at POF Hospital.

Facilities provided by the Deptt

  • Maintenance / repair of all Electromedical equipment installed in hospital and dispensaries including of POF Sanjwal & Havelian.
  • To keep a record of repair/maintenance of all equipment along with their log books.
