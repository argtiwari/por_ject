About Dental Centre

In 1968, Dental Centre POF Hospital was established to provide basic dental treatments to POF employees and their families. The Dental Centre was affiliated with POF Hospital Wah for provision of medicines and admission purpose. In 2004, the new building of Dental Centre was constructed, and it was shifted to a new place.

Since 10 January 2004, the Dental Centre is working as a 05-chair Dental Centre. The Dental Centre is capable of providing all sorts of basic dental treatments, including operative procedures, minor oral surgeries, minor prosthetic repair work, removable orthodontic, and perio procedures.

The Dental Centre is equipped with modern dental equipment, including OPG X-Ray, digital X-Ray, digital imaging, state-of-the-art dental units, Class B Autoclave, glass bead sterilizer, dry heat sterilizer, UV light halogen lamp, high-speed electric motor, apex locator, rotary system, and intraoral camera system.

In 2014, the following latest dental equipment was installed in Dental Centre POF Hospital:

  • Fully Electronic Fiber Optic Dental Unit.
  • Wall-mounted dental X-Ray Unit.
  • Intra Oral Camera System.
  • Digital Apex Locator.
  • Endo Rotary System.
  • 2 x Dental units are under procurement and will be installed soon.
